Doctor Profile

Dr. (Prof.) Amite Pankaj Aggarwal is a prominent orthopedic surgeon known for his extensive experience and expertise in the field of orthopedic surgery. With a career spanning over 25 years, he has gained both national and international recognition for his contributions to joint replacement surgery and orthopedic care. Dr. Aggarwal graduated from the prestigious All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi, where he laid the foundation for his illustrious medical career.

Having successfully performed more than 10,000 joint replacement surgeries, Dr. Aggarwal specializes in complex cases, including those involving significant deformities and revision surgeries. His innovative approach has made him a pioneer in robotic-assisted surgeries, and he is a strong proponent of the "Fast-Track" Total Knee Arthroplasty concept, which emphasizes quick recovery and reduced hospital stay for patients, enhancing their overall surgical experience.

Dr. Aggarwal is not only recognized for his surgical skills but also for his academic accomplishments. He is one of the youngest surgeons in India to be honored with the title of Professor of Orthopedics. His commitment to education is evident in his role as a mentor and faculty member at various regional and national conferences, where he shares his expertise through live surgery demonstrations and teaching sessions.

Qualification and Experience

Dr. Aggarwal's educational background is impressive, reflecting his dedication to the field of orthopedics. His qualifications include:

  • MBBS - All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi
  • MS in Orthopedics - All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi
  • DNB in Orthopedics - National Board of Examinations
  • MRCS - Royal College of Surgeons, Edinburgh
  • MNAMS - Member of the National Academy of Medical Sciences

In addition to his clinical practice, Dr. Aggarwal has participated in numerous research projects and has published extensively in indexed peer-reviewed journals. His academic contributions have earned him recognition and respect within the orthopedic community.

Contributions to Medical Science

Dr. Aggarwal's impact on orthopedic surgery extends beyond his clinical practice. He has made significant contributions to the field through the following:

  • Pioneering robotic-assisted joint replacement surgeries, which enhance precision and outcomes.
  • Promoting the "Fast-Track" approach for knee arthroplasty to facilitate quicker recovery times.
  • Expertise in complex joint replacement surgeries, including revisions and deformities.
  • Conducting advanced arthroscopic procedures, particularly for sports-related injuries and ligament repairs.
  • Training and mentoring upcoming orthopedic surgeons through live surgical demonstrations and cadaveric courses.
  • Serving on editorial boards for reputable orthopedic journals and reviewing articles to maintain high academic standards.

His leadership roles in various orthopedic societies reflect his commitment to advancing the field and improving patient care. Dr. Aggarwal's dedication to utilizing advanced technology in surgery and his continuous pursuit of excellence solidify his reputation as a leading authority in orthopedic surgery, making him a sought-after consultant and educator in the medical community.
