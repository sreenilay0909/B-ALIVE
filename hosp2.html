Fortis Memorial Research Institute (FMRI)

About Fortis Memorial Research Institute

Fortis Memorial Research Institute (FMRI) is a premier multi-specialty hospital situated in Gurgaon, Haryana, India. As a part of the Fortis Healthcare network, one of the largest and most esteemed healthcare providers in the country, FMRI has established itself as a leader in medical excellence. The hospital is celebrated for its state-of-the-art facilities, advanced technology, and commitment to patient-centered care.

Comprehensive Medical Services

FMRI is recognized as a center of excellence in a variety of medical specialties, including but not limited to cardiology, neurology, oncology, orthopedics, gastroenterology, and critical care. The hospital's broad spectrum of medical services ensures that patients have access to comprehensive care tailored to their specific needs.

Expert Medical Team

The hospital boasts a team of highly skilled medical professionals, which includes experienced doctors, specialized super-subspecialists, dedicated nurses, and support staff. These professionals are committed to providing the highest standard of care, utilizing their extensive training and expertise to enhance patient outcomes. Each member of the team is focused on ensuring that patients receive not only effective treatments but also compassionate support throughout their healthcare journey.

Cutting-Edge Technology

FMRI is equipped with some of the most advanced medical technology available today:

  • 256 Slice Brilliance ICT Scanner: This innovative scanner captures detailed images of the entire heart in just two heartbeats, facilitating rapid and accurate diagnosis.
  • Modular Operating Rooms: The hospital features state-of-the-art modular operating rooms furnished with the latest surgical equipment, enhancing the efficiency and safety of surgical procedures.
  • E-ICU: FMRI's pioneering E-ICU incorporates two-way audiovisual communication, allowing clinicians to monitor patients continuously and provide real-time assistance, ensuring optimal care 24/7.
  • Bi-Plane Cath Lab: The ultra-modern Bi-Plane Cath Lab is specifically designed for cardiac and vascular interventions, equipped with the latest technology to support complex procedures.
  • CT-Based Brain Suite: The hospital includes a CT-based Brain Suite equipped with intraoperative CT imaging for brain and spine surgeries, allowing for precision and enhanced surgical outcomes.
