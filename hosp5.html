Max Healthcare Institute

Max Healthcare Institute is one of the leading healthcare organizations in India, primarily based in New Delhi. With a robust network of 14 hospitals spread across North India, Max Healthcare is dedicated to providing high-quality medical care to the communities it serves. The flagship facility, Max Super Specialty Hospital in Saket, was established in 2006 as a key component of the Max Healthcare consortium.

Commitment to Quality Care

Max Healthcare is driven by a mission to deliver superior medical services and enhance the health and well-being of the communities it serves. The organization emphasizes patient-centered care, integrating advanced technology and innovative treatment protocols to ensure that patients receive the best possible outcomes.

Team and Specialties

Max Healthcare employs a dedicated team of more than 2,000 leading doctors, along with 3,300 nurses and 3,200 trained support staff.

Emergency Services

The emergency services at Max Healthcare are designed to provide rapid and efficient care in critical situations.
