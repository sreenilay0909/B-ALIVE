Doctor Profile

Dr. Deepak Kalra is a renowned Nephrologist in New Delhi, India, recognized for his exceptional surgical skills and extensive experience. With over 16 years in the field, he is dedicated to providing safe and efficient care while staying updated with the latest advancements in nephrology. Common conditions he treats include Kidney Failure, Glomerulonephritis, Chronic Kidney Disease, End-Stage Renal Disease, and Polycystic Kidneys.

Qualification and Experience

Currently serving as a Senior Consultant Nephrologist at Fortis Hospital, Shalimar Bagh, New Delhi, Dr. Kalra has accumulated over 23 years of medical experience, with nearly 20 years specializing in nephrology. He holds the following qualifications:

  • DM in Nephrology, 2008 - Sanjay Gandhi Postgraduate Institute of Medical Sciences, Lucknow
  • MD in Medicine, 2002 - King George's Medical University, Lucknow
  • MBBS, 1999 - Chhatrapati Shahu Ji Maharaj University, Kanpur

His areas of expertise include Kidney Transplantation, Clinical Nephrology, and Diabetic Hypertension. He is also a member of the Indian Medical Council.
