Dr. Naresh Trehan

General Information

Experience: 33 Years

Fee: ₹1000

Doctor Profile

Dr. Naresh Trehan is one of India’s most esteemed and experienced cardiovascular and cardiothoracic surgeons, renowned for his pioneering contributions to cardiac surgery. He has been awarded India’s highest civilian honors, the Padma Bhushan and Padma Shri, in recognition of his transformative impact on cardiac care. Dr. Trehan is currently the Chairman and Managing Director of Medanta – The Medicity, a multi-specialty super hospital in Gurgaon, spread over 45 acres, with a 1,500-bed capacity and 45 operating theaters, providing advanced medical services across 20 specialties.

Dr. Trehan graduated from K.G. Medical College, Lucknow, in 1968, and pursued extensive training in general and cardiac surgery at New York University Medical Center. He held several prominent academic and clinical positions in the United States before returning to India to lead advancements in cardiac care. Over his career, he has successfully performed more than 48,000 cardiac surgeries, including complex procedures for coronary artery disease, heart valve surgeries, carotid artery surgeries, and pioneering minimally invasive and robotic-assisted surgeries.

Specialty Interests

Dr. Trehan has significant expertise in a wide range of advanced cardiac surgical procedures, including:

  • Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG): Known for minimally invasive techniques on a beating heart, reducing recovery time and improving patient outcomes.
  • Heart Valve Surgery: Proficient in surgeries involving repair or replacement of cardiac valves to restore proper heart function.
  • Cardiomyoplasty and Aortic Aneurysm Repair: Expert in reconstructive surgeries and procedures to address aortic aneurysms and improve cardiac function.
  • Robotically Assisted Cardiac Surgery: A leader in robotic cardiac surgery, offering precision-driven and minimally invasive options for complex heart surgeries.
  • Advanced Cardiac Procedures: Specializes in transmyocardial laser revascularization, reduction ventriculoplasty (Batista Operation), heart port access, and myocardial total arterial revascularization.

Education

  • Executive Director and Chief Cardiothoracic Surgeon: Escorts Heart Institute and Research Centre, New Delhi (1988–2007)
  • Personal Surgeon: Appointed to the President of India (from 1991)
  • Honorary Consultant: Cromwell Hospital, London, UK (from 1994)
  • Honorary Visiting Professor of Surgery: Bangladesh Medical College, Dhaka (from 2000)
