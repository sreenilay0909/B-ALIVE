Doctor Profile

Dr. Gagan Deep Chhabra is a distinguished nephrologist with over 22 years of experience in nephrology and renal transplantation. He completed his MBBS from PGI Medical College, Rohtak, followed by advanced qualifications including DNB Medicine and DNB Nephrology from top institutions. Known for his expertise in renal care, Dr. Chhabra has performed more than 2500 renal transplants and is highly skilled in vascular access interventions such as AVF (arteriovenous fistulas) and AV shunts.

Dr. Chhabra has practiced at leading hospitals, including Max Super Speciality Hospital, Saket, Sir Ganga Ram Hospital, and B.L.K. Hospital. He has received prestigious recognition, notably the AITSE award, presented by the President of India, for his contributions to the field. In addition to his clinical expertise, he has served as a DNB co-guide and teacher for over eight years, mentoring the next generation of nephrologists.

Qualification and Experience

Dr. Chhabra’s extensive qualifications and dedication to nephrology have solidified his reputation as an expert in renal care. His academic and professional credentials include:

  • MBBS - PGI Medical College, Rohtak
  • DNB in Medicine - Renowned National Institution
  • DNB in Nephrology - Leading National Institution

Dr. Chhabra is recognized not only for his technical skills but also for his patient-centered approach, focusing on providing the highest level of care for those with kidney disease. His areas of expertise include complex kidney transplants, vascular access for dialysis, and comprehensive management of chronic kidney disease (CKD).

Contributions to Medical Science

Throughout his career, Dr. Chhabra has made significant contributions to nephrology and renal transplantation. His specialties include:

  • Renal Transplantation and Post-transplant Care
  • Vascular Access Procedures (AV Fistula and AV Shunts)
  • Management of Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)
  • Dialysis Access Creation and Maintenance
  • Management of Acute Kidney Injury (AKI)
  • Hypertension Management in Renal Disease
  • Electrolyte Imbalance Treatment
  • Polycystic Kidney Disease (PKD) Treatment
  • Infectious Disease Management in Immunosuppressed Patients

Dr. Chhabra’s contributions have been instrumental in advancing the field of nephrology, especially in renal transplant surgery and post-operative care. His ongoing dedication to research and teaching continues to impact the field positively.
