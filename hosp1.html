Medanta - The Medicity

About Medanta - The Medicity

Medanta - The Medicity is a multi-super specialty hospital located in Gurgaon, India. Founded in 2009 by renowned cardiac surgeon Dr. Naresh Trehan, the hospital offers a comprehensive range of medical and surgical services, including cardiology, oncology, neurology, orthopedics, and more. With state-of-the-art facilities and a team of highly experienced medical professionals, Medanta is considered one of the leading hospitals in India. The hospital also has a strong commitment to research and education, with various programs aimed at improving patient care and advancing the field of medicine. Overall, Medanta is known for its high standards of patient care and dedication to providing top-quality medical services to people in India and around the world.

Team and Specialities

Medanta - The Medicity comprises a highly educated and experienced team of practitioners, clinical researchers, and scientists committed to discovering new technologies and ideas. This multidisciplinary team fosters collaborations that advance diagnostic, therapeutic, and preventive services, enabling cutting-edge patient care.

The hospital brings together an outstanding pool of doctors, multidisciplinary researchers, scientists, and clinical experts to inspire new ideas, discover new technologies, and translate scientific advances swiftly into new methods of diagnosing, treating, and preventing diseases.

Led by Dr. A.S. Soin, Medanta’s team performed the first successful intestinal transplant in India in January 2013. Their team has also achieved numerous milestones, including:

  • Performing over 15,000 cardiac surgeries and 2,500 joint replacement surgeries.
  • Conducting the highest number of liver transplants in India, and the second-highest globally, with over 500 living donor liver transplants.
  • Hosting an American Heart Association-certified International Training Centre for heart care.

Medanta offers integrated, customized training programs for doctors, nurses, paramedical staff, and community members. The facility includes training rooms, a library, a simulation lab with access to digital resources, audiovisual training aids, and expert full-time faculty, all aimed at enhancing knowledge and skill in healthcare.
