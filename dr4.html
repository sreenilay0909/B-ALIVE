Doctor Profile

Dr. Arvind Kumar is a seasoned and renowned medical professional with over 35 years of rich experience. Holding an MBBS and MD from the prestigious All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi, Dr. Kumar provides exemplary care to infants, children, and adolescents. Recognized widely for his dedication, he is known for offering comprehensive medical help across a broad range of pediatric illnesses, including emergency medicine, infectious diseases, and pediatric endocrinology.

Dr. Kumar is also distinguished by his numerous research publications in both national and international journals. He is a sought-after expert in his field, having appeared on various healthcare-focused TV shows, and his pioneering work on identifying a rare disorder related to obesity was even covered on the Discovery Channel.

Qualification and Experience

As an accomplished pediatrician, Dr. Kumar’s extensive qualifications and nearly four decades of experience have solidified his reputation as one of the leading specialists in pediatric healthcare. His academic credentials include:

  • MBBS - All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi
  • MD in Pediatrics - All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi

Dr. Kumar's areas of expertise include handling complex cases of infectious diseases, pediatric endocrinology, and emergency care, as well as preventative medicine. His deep knowledge and vast experience make him a trusted figure in the field of pediatric healthcare.
